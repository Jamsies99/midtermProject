You lunge for the cosmopolitan, but it vanishes into thin air! You see in it's place:

a glass,

a cocktail shaker,

a strainer

.25 ounce of simple syrup,

.5 ounce of lime juice,

1 ounce of cranberry juice,

2 ounces of vodka,

.5 ounce of triple sec,

an orange peel,

and ice cubes.

You grab the cocktail shaker and deposit the ice, simple syrup, lime juice, cranberry juice, vodka and triple sec into the shaker's confines. Slamming on the top of the shaker, you vigoriously shake for 10 seconds.

You take off the top of the shaker to reveal the treasure nested within. This vibrant fluid with it's sweet alluring aroma, is temptious in nature. Yet this treasure that you have unearthed is still unrefined.

To further exemplify the promiscious and elegant nature, this mixture is filtered with a strainer, leaving behind the fragments of ice and bringing forward a smooth drink that is sweet and playful, yet balanced.

You taste the drink, and although concoction is amazing, the aroma is overpoweringly sweet. To balance the scent, you squeeze an orange peel over top.
